Our Detector

Our spectrometer is equipped with an electron multiplier detector. This is a standard detector in spectrometry, which gives a high resolution of 1.1 x 106 pulses/sec for each sample analyzed. The use of a multiplier helps boost the signal from ions passing through the instrument, which can allow for detection of contaminants even if there is low ionization due to the lack of a matrix and small sample size.

Peace of mind in every piece of food

Have you had problems with achieving a decent USFDA food score? How many people had to be sent to the emergency room from your restaurant due to allergens? If you are concerned after any of these questions, our stovetop spectrometer is ready for analysis in any food-distributing restaurant.

Our Mission

Quality

Our mass analyzer being an orbitrap with a maximum resolution (FWHM) of 1,000,000 at m/z 200 and a sub-1 ppm mass accuracy makes our product compact but effective. Then our novel laser desorption ionization (LDI) system can achieve a spatial resolution of 20 μm.

Functionality

This novel instrument can use its LDI to ionize the food of interest at multiple different times during the cooking process. Ions are then introduced into the instrument and are sent to an orbitrap for separation by mass over charge ratio. Lastly, an electron multiplier gives a high resolution of 1.1 x 106 pulses/sec for each sample analyzed.

Reliability

With a lifetime warranty and years of improving manufacturing, our stove top spectrometer is the best of its kind. If the lifetime warranty needs to be utilized, we as the FDA promise to work as fast as possible to resolve your issue, unlike SCIEX…

Compatibility

Our design has allowed for compatibility with any stovetop. From gas to electric and two burners to ten, our team is ready for any challenge. If the worry about food compatibility has crossed your mind, do not be afraid, our innovative design can analyze all frogs from soups to brownies!

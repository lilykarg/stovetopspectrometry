Our Ion Source

We strive to offer our clients a reliable instrument that operates in real time. That's why the Stovetop Spectrometer comes equipped with a Laser Desorption Ionization source.

How it Works

The LDI provides soft ionization over an extensive surface area, making it compatible with a variety of dishes. It requires no harmful matrix material to be added to food items, cutting down on cost and allowing sampled and non-contaminated food to remain edible. Pulses are supplied every 10-30 seconds (optimized according to cooking method and/or dish) as the cooking progresses, which allows for not only the detection of contaminants but tracking of their introduction and movement over time.

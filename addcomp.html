Additional Components

Adjustable head

The Stovetop Spectrometer is equipped with an adjustable head for the LDI ion source to allow for use with various dishes and pots and pans of every size. We strive to make our products easy to use and versatile without the need for additional parts and expensive upgrades. Additionally, the adjustable head folds in for easy transport of the unit

Splash Shield

Our stovetop model comes equipped with a quartz splash guard on the base of our LDI head. We know that cooking can get messy, and this component keeps the stovetop spectrometer clean as possible without limiting the effectiveness of the laser.

Pre-Programmed Monitoring Methods

Worried about a fancy instrument being in your kitchen? No need! Our software has multiple methods pre-programmed to run with ease, can be started up with the push of a button, and will notify you if one of the contaminants on the selected analyte list is detected.

Available Methods

  • Biological contaminants: E. coli, salmonella, and more
  • Human allergens (plant-based): peanuts, tree nuts, gluten, and more
  • Human allergens (animal-based): shellfish, lactose, and more
  • Pharmaceutical and pesticide contaminats
